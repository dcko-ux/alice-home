# Alice SMS Notification Opt-In

Alice is a privately operated home-assistant application managed by
REGISTERED BRAND NAME.

SMS service is limited to the application owner and specifically authorized
members of the owner's household. It is not offered to the public.

A household member opts in by directly asking the owner to enable Alice SMS
notifications for that person's mobile number. Before adding the number, the
owner explains that:

- Messages are automated home-assistant notifications and responses.
- Message frequency varies based on household events and requests.
- Message and data rates may apply.
- Consent is optional and is not a condition of purchasing anything.
- Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
- Reply HELP for assistance.

After receiving explicit consent, the owner manually adds the recipient's
mobile number to Alice's private configuration. Alice does not scrape,
purchase, rent, or obtain phone numbers from third parties.

Mobile information and SMS consent will not be shared with third parties or
affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
